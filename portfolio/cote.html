Notre Histoire

From the shores of the
Riviera to the world

Born from a summer spent wandering the coastline between Cannes and Monaco, CÔTE was created for those who understand that true luxury is not loud. It whispers in the way linen falls, in the weight of silk against warm skin, in the unhurried grace of a woman walking barefoot on warm stone.

Each piece is conceived in our atelier in Nice and crafted by artisans who have spent generations mastering their craft.
